Radio 1's Residency

Cajmere aka Green Velvet drops a deep Guest Mix and Derrick Carter picks his Classic Corner record for the week!

Cajmere aka Green Velvet drops a deep and loved up Guest Mix and speaks about his new album, his weirdest DJ experience and dream collaborations!

Meanwhile Derrick Carter, brings his inaugural Classic Corner track, representing the old school Chicago sound.

New upfront records from the likes of Michael Gracioppo, Kink, DJ Sneak and Bicep! Fierce!
